    How TwitchPoint farms

    Two independent credit pipelines run side by side:

    Drops — the picked drop channel is owned exclusively by the drops Watcher, sending GraphQL sendSpadeEvents heartbeats every ~59 seconds and polling DropCurrentSession every minute. Auto-claim fires when a drop hits 100%.

    Channel-Points — up to 2 rotation channels are watched at a time via the legacy POST spade.twitch.tv/track endpoint. Bonus claims (the chest icon) are auto-claimed via PubSub. Rotation cycles through online channels every 5 minutes.

    Priority: P0 (auto, drop-active channels) → P1 (always-watch) → P2 (rotate). The drops Watcher's current channel is skipped by points rotation to avoid double-tracking.

    Drop campaigns marked [Auto] are farmed automatically because the account is linked — they're not in your wanted_games priority list. With an empty wanted_games list, every linked campaign is auto-discovered and the marker is hidden.
